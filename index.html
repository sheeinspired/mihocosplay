About Miho

A passion for transforming imagination into reality.

Miho is a cosplayer from the Philippines who has been bringing beloved anime and game characters to life since 2012.

What began as a love for anime and gaming became a creative journey filled with artistry, performance, travel, competitions, and meaningful connections throughout the cosplay community.
